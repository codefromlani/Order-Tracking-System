Welcome to the Order Tracking System

This platform allows you to efficiently track orders, monitor shipments, manage product availability, and keep track of invoices in real-time.

Key Features

  • Track product availability and stock levels.
  • Place and monitor orders in real-time.
  • View order statuses: pending, shipped, delivered, etc.
  • Manage shipments and track delivery status.
  • Keep track of invoices and expenses.
